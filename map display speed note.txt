Which is faster to display, wide rectangles or tall rectangles?
10000 blocks total. (displayed 100 times each.)

  H x W		average display time
---------------------------------------------
200 x 50	: 1.110540 secs	
100 x 100 	: 1.201080 secs (~ 9% slower)
 50 x 200 	: 1.533310 secs (~38% slower)

So wide maps display slower than tall ones.
